NothingIsNeutral

A digital collection of thoughts on design

Chapter01

SEEK CALMCOLOR.

This piece starts in the emotional and aesthetic realm. It explores how design can soothe and comfort with gradients as a language of feeling. It’s about personal, sensory, and human side of design like what we feel before we think.

Chapter02

EXPOSE HIDDENPOWER.

This one transitions from feeling to thinking. It reveals that design isn’t neutral and every choice encodes power, privilege, and perspective. It asks designers to become aware of the systems their work participates in.

Chapter03

RESISTEASYANSWERS.

This closes the loop by questioning the system of design itself. It challenges the myth of universal solutions and calls for humility, context and critical thinking. Where the fist is emotional, the second ideological, the third is philosophical and ethical.

Designed by Noor as part of an exploration in design thinking
